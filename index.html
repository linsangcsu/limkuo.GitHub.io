About Me

  • Lin Guo is currently a Visiting Research Student at the University of Victoria (UVic), Victoria, BC, Canada, under the supervision of Prof. Jianping Pan. He is also a Ph.D. student at School of Computer Science and Engineering, Central South University, Changsha, P. R. China, with Prof. Zhigang Chen.
  • His research interests include modeling and performance optimization in green sensor/IoT networks. He is a student member of IEEE.
